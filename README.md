# Vicenza Development Investment Joint Stock Company

## About Us

Vicenza Development Investment Joint Stock Company is a technology-driven enterprise specializing in industrial automation, software development, and hardware engineering. We focus on creating integrated solutions for production lines, monitoring systems, and enterprise management.

## Our Core Expertise

Our technical ecosystem spans across multiple domains to ensure seamless industrial operations:

- **Industrial Automation**: Specialized firmware and hardware development for manufacturing machinery (e.g., brick making machines, production lines).
- **Software Solutions**: Custom enterprise software including CRM systems, attendance tracking (`checkchamcong`), and internal evaluation tools.
- **IoT & Monitoring**: Advanced power and internet monitoring systems, server health tracking, and ESP-NOW communication protocols.
- **Infrastructure**: Scalable web applications and weighing bridge management systems.

## Repository Naming Convention

To maintain clarity across our 14+ repositories, we follow a strict prefixing system:

- `firmware-`: Embedded systems and microcontroller code.
- `hardware-`: Circuit designs and mechanical specifications.
- `software-`: Desktop or backend application logic.
- `website-`: Web-based platforms and portals.
- `vector-`: Design assets and graphical resources.
